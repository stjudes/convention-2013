April 19-21, 2013
Belgrave Heights Convention Centre

A weekend away in beautiful Belgrave Heights with Time to Focus on our great God, our relationships with each other and our mission to the World! These are the themes of the St Jude's Convention 2013, our chance to gather people from all of our 7 congregations together to hear from God's Word, to sing His praises, to enjoy each other and to have great fun.

  • Richard Condie

  • Michael Raiter

Pricing and registration

  • God and His Word

    We will be hearing both Richard Condie (Vicar, St Judes) and Michael Raiter (Director, Centre for Biblical Preaching) speaking from the Bible in a collection of 'A Time to Focus' sessions. John Anderson (former Deputy Prime Minister & Christian speaker) will continue this theme in a talk on 'A Time to Focus on the World.'

  • Program

    Arriving Friday night, from Saturday morning a program of talks will discuss the topic 'Time to Focus' with an emphasis on God, each other, the World and St Judes. On Saturday evening we will enjoy a celebration dinner, and on Sunday morning there will be a communion service. Youth, primary, and pre-school programs will be run for all morning talks except the communion service.

  • Accomodation

    Beginning Friday night and ending Sunday afternoon, many will camp in the park-like grounds of BHC, while others will stay in lodges or their own offsite B&B accommodation. There will be plenty of free time to enjoy the hills, and each other’s company. See below.

  • Families

    We cater for the whole family! There will be programs and care provided for all children 2 years old and up. There is a large Parent’s Room with vision and sound of the main sessions for parents with children under 2 years old. There will even be activities for children on the Saturday afternoon.

  • Camping

    150 powered sites - bring your own tent or caravan and all bedding. Fun and adventure for all!

  • Cabins / A-frames

    The Cabins and A-Frames are dorm style accommodation. Cabins (suitable for families or singles) have single bunks and the A-Frames (suitable for singles) have mattresses on the floor. Please bring your own bedding.

  • Diamond Valley

    13 cabins of various sizes. Suitable for families or single sex groups. Please bring your own bedding.

  • The Lodge

    The Lodge is fully furnished to sleep 52 people in shared rooms, with double, single or bunk, beds. It is a short distance from the main convention site, with its own bathroom facilities. Suitable for families, couples and singles. All bedding is provided
